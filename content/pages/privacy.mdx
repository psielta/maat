---
title: Privacy
description: Privacy notes for Maat.
---

Maat stores only the data needed to run the task workspace.

## Account data

The app stores your email address and optional profile fields created by the authentication flow.

## Workspace data

Boards, lists, cards, descriptions, and board memberships are persisted so collaborators can work on the same environment.

## Email

Development email is delivered through MailHog. Production email is intended to use Resend.

## Billing

Maat is free and does not include billing, subscriptions, or Stripe integration.
