Maat is a free portfolio application for personal task management and collaborative Kanban workspaces.

## Use

Use Maat to organize personal software development work, share boards with other users, and test realtime collaboration flows.

## Accounts

Authentication is email-based. Keep access to your email account secure, because sign-in links are delivered there.

## Availability

This project is maintained as a portfolio application. Features may change while the app evolves.

## Data

Boards, lists, cards, and workspace memberships are stored in PostgreSQL. Realtime notifications are transient and delivered through Redis while users are connected.
